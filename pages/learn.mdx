# Learn

Learn screen shows flashcards or carousel of words (term and definition). They eye icon button will toggle show and hide definition part. Interval is the carousel play interval. The cycling icon button toggle cycling play mode. Shuffle button will re-shuffle the words. Play button will play the carousel.
